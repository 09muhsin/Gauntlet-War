## The reality check before we start

1,000 orders. 30 days. $15k. DTC only. Trust Play.

Here's the honest problem with Trust Play: it pays slow. SEO won't rank a pillar page in 30 days — it ranks it in month 4. So we don't pretend. We run a hybrid. The trust assets (pillar, nurture, PR) do the *closing*. Paid buys the eyeballs we can't earn organically in four weeks, then hands them to a credibility machine that converts them. The articles keep selling long after the launch ends. Months 2–6 are where this gets cheap.

## PER-CHANNEL PLAYBOOK

**SEO / Long-form**
- *Format:* One pillar + 3 support articles, 1,500–2,500 words, each answering a real foot-pain search and linking to the product.
- *Cadence:* Pillar Day 6, then one support piece per week.
- *Hook formula:* Kill the shame ("not your fitness — your shoe at mile two") → name the three causes → resolve with the product.
- *Proof metric:* Organic clicks to product page + assisted conversions. Won't peak in 30 days — this is the month 2–6 engine.

**Email Nurture**
- *Format:* 5-email welcome flow + 3-email abandoned-cart + weekly broadcast.
- *Cadence:* New leads enter the flow on signup; one broadcast a week.
- *Hook formula:* Open on her exact moment (6:15am, arch on the kitchen floor, the Sunday-night app stare) → relief → risk-reversal CTA.
- *Proof metric:* Click-to-buy from email. Watch open rate ≥40%, click ≥6%.

**PR Outreach**
- *Format:* Personal pitch + free seeded pairs to runners/writers; podcast guest spots.
- *Cadence:* 40 outreach Day 5; follow-ups Weeks 2–3; podcasts Weeks 3–4.
- *Hook formula:* The contrarian wedge — "the brand that banned 'feels great in the store.'"
- *Proof metric:* # of real testimonials/mentions collected. These feed paid + landing trust directly.

**Paid (the spark — funds reach Trust Play can't earn in 30 days)**
- *Format:* Founder video + statics + testimonial creative. Meta primary, Google retargeting secondary.
- *Cadence:* Live Day 4, scale winners daily, refresh creative weekly.
- *Hook formula:* Her words → "mile two" pain → 60-day risk-free test.
- *Proof metric:* ROAS (target ≥2.5 to stay profitable at $120 AOV) and cost-per-purchase ≤$30.

## RISKS & FALLBACKS

**1. Testimonials don't come back in time.** The whole "we sell mile three" claim needs real proof or it's a slogan. *Move:* If seeded runners go quiet by Day 10, pay $50 gift cards (from the $800 fund) to the first 20 verified buyers for an honest before/after of their loop. Lead with the founder's own logged miles until reviews land. Never fabricate a name.

**2. Paid CAC runs hot (>$30/order).** At $120 AOV that eats margin fast and stalls the ramp. *Move:* Cut to retargeting + email only, lean harder on PR/podcast reach (cheaper trust), shift the angle to the 60-day guarantee. High CAC usually means low confidence — so we add proof and reverse risk, not spend more.

**3. We fall short of 1,000 by Day 25.** *Move:* Real scarcity close on first-batch sizes (only if true) + a final broadcast to the full nurture list. If we're still short, here's the honest read: Trust Play seeded a list of 2,400 warm leads and 4 ranking articles that keep selling in month 2. That's a base, not a one-month miracle. Report the compounding curve, not just the Day-30 number.
