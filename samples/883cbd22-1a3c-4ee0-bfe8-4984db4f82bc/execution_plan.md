# EXECUTION PLAN — "Wrong Test" Campaign
### Career Coaching on Multiple Intelligence · $0 budget · 6 months · Goal: 1,000 orders/enquiries

> **THE BIG IDEA:** *You're not lost. You were tested on the wrong intelligence. School checked two kinds of smart. There are eight. You've spent your whole life judged on the two that aren't yours.*

Everything below ladders to that one line. If a post doesn't connect to "Wrong Test," cut it.

A note on the team. I'm assuming three pairs of hands: **Founder** (on camera, the voice, the calls), **Editor** (cuts video, schedules, posts), **Ops** (DMs, email, tracking sheet, community). Solo? Then you ARE all three — do the day in that order and protect the 2-videos-a-day floor above everything else.

One rule before we start: **post twice a day, every day, and let the library compound.** That's the whole game. Budget isn't the risk. Stopping is.

## PART 3 — PER-CHANNEL PLAYBOOK

### TikTok + Instagram Reels (same cut, both platforms)
- **Format:** 20–40 sec. Founder face, no music for the first 2 sec (the pattern interrupt). Big on-screen caption. Often a screen-record of a real result in the back half.
- **Cadence:** 2/day, every day. One before noon, one evening.
- **Hook formula:** *[contrarian line that names their shame] → [the two-vs-eight reframe] → [name one overlooked intelligence as a vivid scene] → "45 min, free, comment LANE."*
- **What "working" looks like:** **3-second hold rate** (are strangers staying past the hook?) and **saves**. Saves predict reach better than likes. Get one saved a lot? Make three more like it.
- **Link rule:** never a link in the video. Drive to "comment LANE → DM the link." Beats the outbound-link penalty and builds a warm DM list.

### LinkedIn
- **Format:** text story, 120–200 words, first line is a scene not a thesis. One CTA: free assessment in comments. Carousel once a week.
- **Cadence:** 1/weekday.
- **Hook formula:** *[exact timestamped dread — "Sunday, 7:04pm"] → [the good-job-wrong-life confession] → [the wrong-lane reframe] → free assessment in comments.*
- **What "working" looks like:** **comments where strangers tell their own story** (not "great post"). That's buying intent. Plus profile-clicks-to-link.

### Micro-influencer / affiliate ($0 cash, rev-share only)
- **Format:** creator takes the real assessment, posts their genuine result + affiliate link, earns a cut per converted enquiry.
- **Cadence:** recruit ~5/month from Week 6; aim for 10–15 active by Week 12.
- **The pitch (DM, copy-paste):**
  > "Hey [name] — your audience is the 'good job, wrong life' crowd, exactly. No cash ask. Take our 45-min assessment, post your real result, get a cut on every coaching enquiry from your link. You only earn when it works. Want your free assessment first, so you can see it's real?"
- **What "working" looks like:** **enquiries per active creator.** One who sends 20 beats ten who send zero — find the pattern in who works and recruit more of them.

### Email (the conversion engine)
- **Format:** 5-email sequence fired on completion, plus weekly broadcasts to non-converters.
- **Cadence:** sequence is automatic; 1 broadcast/week from Week 13.
- **Hook formula:** *[name the discomfort in their result] → [reframe the call as low-risk] → "Reply READY" → PS that kills the no-time/fear objection.*
- **What "working" looks like:** **READY reply rate** (finisher → enquiry). This is the lever that decides whether you hit 1,000.

### Community (Discord / Facebook, Week 13+)
- **Format:** free group, every finisher invited. Weekly "top intelligence + stuck job" thread; monthly live Q&A.
- **Cadence:** 1 prompt/week, 1 live/month.
- **What "working" looks like:** **% of enquiries from members vs cold.** Community referrals convert highest — watch that share climb.

## PART 4 — FUNNEL TARGETS (weekly ramp to 1,000)

The math: organic content → free assessment (~2–4% of engaged viewers finish) → ~12% of finishers raise their hand (the enquiry). To land ~1,000 enquiries we need ~8,000 completed assessments — which needs the reach to build across 6 months as the library and creators compound.

| Phase | Weeks | Engaged reach/wk | Assessments started/wk | Completed/wk | Enquiries/wk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 Find winners | 2–4 | 8k → 20k | 200 → 450 | 130 → 300 | 16 → 36 |
| 2 Scale + creators | 5–12 | 25k → 70k | 550 → 1,400 | 360 → 950 | 45 → 115 |
| 3 Email/community | 13–20 | 70k → 95k | 1,500 → 1,900 | 1,000 → 1,300 | 130 → 175 |
| 4 Compound | 21–26 | 90k → 110k | 1,800 → 2,100 | 1,250 → 1,450 | 165 → 195 |

**Cumulative enquiries by end of month:** M1 ~25 · M2 ~100 · M3 ~250 · M4 ~480 · M5 ~760 · M6 ~1,050. The back half does most of the work — by design. Don't panic in month 1.

## PART 6 — RISKS & FALLBACKS

**1. No video pops for 2–3 weeks.** Likeliest stall. The hook's too slow or too clever.
**Move:** cut the first 2 seconds down to one confession — "You're not lazy." Strip the music. Lead with the most uncomfortable line in the script. Test 5 brand-new first lines in 3 days, same payoff underneath. One in fifteen pops — you just haven't shipped the fifteenth yet. Keep posting.

**2. Assessments started but not finished (45 min is the friction).**
**Move:** add a video that pre-sells the 45: "Yeah, it's 45 minutes. So's one bad meeting. Difference is, this one tells you why you keep landing in those meetings." Then fire a same-day "you're 12 minutes from your result" nudge to abandoners.

**3. People finish but don't reply READY (traffic's fine, enquiries stall).**
**Move:** it's the email, not the videos. Rewrite Email #2 around the single result line they can't stop chewing on. Lean harder on the PS objection-kill — "you're not scared of the call, you're scared it'll confirm you're stuck." And drop in a real testimonial the second you have one. Proof breaks the no-confidence wall faster than any line I can write.
